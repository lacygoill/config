Msg: `$` is useless to evaluate a variable used as an index when slicing a string.

# Bad:

    var='value'
    i=1
    j=2
    echo "${var:$i:$j}"
                ^  ^

# Good:

    var='value'
    i=1
    j=2
    echo "${var:i:j}"
                ^ ^

# Rationale:

It's also more consistent with how  we omit the dollar sigil inside `(( ... ))`,
and in the subscript of an associative array.

# Regex:

    ${[^ ]*:\$
