Msg: `$(< file)` is faster than `$(cat file)`.

# Bad:

    $(cat file)
      ^^^

# Good:

    $(< file)
      ^

# Rationale:

   > The command substitution  $(cat file) can be replaced by  the equivalent but
   > faster $(< file).

Source: `man bash /EXPANSION/;/Command Substitution/;/$(<`

# Regex:

    $(\s*\zscat\s\f
