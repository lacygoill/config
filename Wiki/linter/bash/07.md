Msg: `readonly` is more readable than `declare -r`.

# Bad:

    declare -r VAR=value
    ^--------^

# Good:

    readonly VAR=value
    ^------^

# Regex:
```vim
    '\%(^\s*\)\@<='
    .. '\%(declare\|local\)\>'
    # assert the presence of the readonly attribute
    .. '\%([-aA[:blank:]]\+-r\>\)\@='
    # assert the absence of the integer attribute (because `readonly` doesn't support `-i`)
    .. '\%([-aAr[:blank:]]\+-i\>\)\@!'
```
